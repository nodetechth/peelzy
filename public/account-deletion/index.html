Privacy controls

Delete your Peelzy account

Account and associated data deletion request

Send the request from the email address registered to your Peelzy account so we can verify ownership.

How to request deletion

  1. Email peelzy@nodetech.jp from your registered account email.
  2. Use the subject line Peelzy Account Deletion Request.
  3. Include the email address registered to your Peelzy account. Do not send your password.
  4. We may ask for limited additional information if needed to verify account ownership.
Request account deletion

Data that will be deleted

  • Your Peelzy account and authentication identifiers.
  • Stickers, thumbnails, books, notes, stamps, and page layouts associated with the account.
  • Exchange offers, proposals, and related account-linked exchange information.
  • Cloud-stored files associated with your account.

Processing time and retention

Verified requests are normally completed within 30 days. Residual copies may remain in protected backups for up to 90 days. Limited billing, fraud-prevention, security, or legal records may be retained longer where required by law or reasonably necessary.

Subscriptions

Deleting your Peelzy account does not automatically cancel an app-store subscription. Cancel an active subscription through Google Play or Apple account settings to prevent future renewal.
